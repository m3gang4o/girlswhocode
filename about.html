Frequently Asked Questions

What does GWC stand for?
GWC stands for Girls Who Code, an organization that supports young women in tech. We are committed to helping people learn about the wonderful world of tech!
How can I join the program?
You can navigate to our Get Involved page above or contact our team.
Do I need coding experience?
No prior experience is needed! We welcome all skill levels and have different levels of classes depending on your experience.
What programming languages and topics will I learn?
In the beginner level classes, you will learn the basics of programming with Turtles and in our advanced classes, you will code with Python.
Is there a membership fee?
No, the organization is free for any member to join!
